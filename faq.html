Frequently Asked Questions

Can somebody who is under the age of 18 ever check in? +
Yes, if they have a parent or guardian present, they can check in under their supervision. They can only remain in the building as long as the guardian is present. Residents cannot check in guests under 18 themselves, except under special exceptions made by WKU Housing management.
How long can a guest stay in the dorm? +
No longer than three days before they get charged for cohabitation. A common workaround is to suggest one day on, one day off if a resident frequently checks in a significant other or partner.
How much does it cost for a spare key if I lose my original key? +
The cost is $45 for the room change and door core replacement. Both you and your roommate (if applicable) will need to pick up new keys at the desk after returning your old ones.
Do I have to go home for breaks such as summer break, fall break, winter break, or spring break? +
Yes, unless you live in an extended living hall (currently PFT, Meredith Hall, and Zacharias Hall).
What happens if I lose my ID? +
If a resident loses their ID, report them on the "No ID Form." Advise them to either find their ID or visit the ID office in Downing Student Union to get a replacement. If they don’t have their ID, ask for their name, birthday, and 800 number to confirm their residency.
Can food deliveries be held at the desk? +
No. Residents must meet delivery drivers outside or let them into the building themselves. Desk staff cannot hold food deliveries or allow delivery personnel into the building.
What should I do if I don’t know the answer to a resident’s question? +
If it’s urgent, call the RA or Hall Director on-call (depending on the time of day). If it’s not urgent, refer them to additional resources or ask if they want you to contact someone on-call.
Can I share information about residents with parents or guardians? +
No. Due to FERPA regulations, you cannot share information about residents with parents or guardians. Hall Directors and RAs can perform wellness checks but cannot disclose personal details.
Do residence halls provide trash bags and recycling bags? +
Yes. Residents can receive up to three trash bags and three recycling bags per day.
How do I check someone into the building as a guest under a resident? +
Swipe the resident’s ID in StarRez.
Select "New" from the dropdown menu and scroll down to "Visitor."
If the visitor is already registered:
- Select "Please select a visitor."
- Swipe their ID (if they are a student) or search by name using their government-issued ID.
If they are not registered:
- Click "Create New Visitor."
- Enter their first and last name along with any other required information.
- Click "OK."
